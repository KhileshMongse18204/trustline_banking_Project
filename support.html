Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a TrustLine account?

Creating a TrustLine account is simple and takes just 5 minutes. Download the app, enter your email and phone number, set a secure password, and verify your identity. Once verified, you can start using TrustLine immediately.

Is TrustLine safe and secure?

Yes, absolutely. TrustLine uses bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring to protect your account and transactions. We comply with all RBI guidelines and undergo regular security audits.

What are the transaction fees?

TrustLine offers zero-fee UPI transfers and low-cost international transfers. We provide complete transparency with no hidden charges. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm a transaction.

How long do transfers take?

Most UPI transfers are instant and reach the recipient in seconds. Bank transfers typically complete within 1-2 business days. International transfers may take 2-5 business days depending on the destination country.

Can I link multiple bank accounts?

Yes, you can link multiple bank accounts and wallets to your TrustLine account. Switch between accounts easily for different transactions. All connections are secured with encryption.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your registered email or phone number, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. You may need to verify your identity for security purposes.

How do I report a suspicious transaction?

Contact our support team immediately through live chat, email, or phone. Report the transaction details, and our fraud team will investigate within 24 hours. You can also file a complaint through the app.
